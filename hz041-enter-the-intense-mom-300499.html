HZ041: Enter the Intense Mom!

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  85 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
Dot's mother Blanca visits the Brave Asagi. It appears she's there to pick up Dot, who's only been on the ship on a trial basis... Liko and Roy want to make her stay, so they decide to ask her how she truly feels about all of this. It's time to reveal the never-before-told story of how Dot joined the Rising Volt Tacklers!
