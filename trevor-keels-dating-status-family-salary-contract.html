Inside Trevor Keels Family Life

Trevor was born to parents Roland Keels and Keyosha Keels on 26 August 2003. He goes by the full name, Trevor Jamaal Keels.

The 19-year-old’s father works for the federal government as a budget analyst. He has got supportive parents who have sacrificed much for his career. Moreover, his mother often shares posts demonstrating her pride in her son’s achievements.

Keels is the third of four children of his parents. He has two elder brothers Trent Walker and Trevon Heard, and a younger sister named Raelyn Keels. He grew up with his family in Clinton, Maryland.

His NFL Journey

Keels played high school basketball at Paul VI Catholic High School in Chantilly, Virginia. There, he recorded 28.7 points, 9.1 rebounds, 7.2 assists, and 3.8 steals per contest as a senior.

After graduating, he went to Duke University to play college basketball. He played for the team for a season, averaging 11.5 points per game to go with 3.4 rebounds and 2.7 assists.


Trevor was selected by The New York Knicks with the 42nd overall pick in the 2022 NBA draft. Meanwhile, he is on a two-way contract with the Westchester Knicks of the NBA G League.

Trevor Keels Salary & Contract

Keels earns a salary of about $509K, playing for the Knicks. He penned a two-way contract on 10 July 2022.
