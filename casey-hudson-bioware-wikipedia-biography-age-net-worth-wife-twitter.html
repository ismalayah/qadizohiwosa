Casey Hudson is a video game developer known for his work on several BioWare video games, most notably the Mass Effect trilogy as a game director based on a third-person RPG.

After completing advanced research, Casey joined Canadian video game developer Bioware, where he is considered a technical artist in games including MDK-2. He eventually returned to Bioware and was announced in 2017. He succeeded Aryn Flynn as General Manager of the studio.
