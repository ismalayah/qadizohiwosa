Caitlin (Japanese: カトレア Cattleya) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Plasma Blast expansion.

Card text

Plasma Blast print
Put as many cards from your hand as you like on the bottom of your deck in any order. Then, draw a card for each card you put on the bottom of your deck.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).
Chilling Reign print
Put any number of cards from your hand on the bottom of your deck in any order. Then, draw that many cards.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn.

Release information

This card was included in the Plasma Blast expansion with artwork by Yusuke Ohmura, first released in the Japanese Megalo Cannon expansion. In Japan, it was reprinted as a Mirror Holofoil BW-P Promotional card and was one of ten cards available in Promotional Card Packs distributed at venues hosting Gym Challenge events from April 2013. This print features the Gym Challenge logo. It was reprinted as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Chilling Reign expansion, first released in the Japanese Jet-Black Spirit expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Sanosuke Sakuma, while the Full Art and Rainbow Rare prints feature artwork by En Morikura.

Trivia

Origin

Caitlin is a member of the Unova Elite Four. Caitlin's room as it appears in Pokémon Black 2 and White 2 is featured in the Plasma Blast print's illustration, while her room as it appears in Pokémon Black and White is featured in the Chilling Reign Regular print's illustration.
