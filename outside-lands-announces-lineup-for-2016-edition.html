Outside Lands is hammering out the details for its 2016 edition, and its made an announcement to get its attendees up to speed. The San Fransisco festival’s organizers have dropped a lineup that’s chock full of names spanning every music genre on the spectrum.

Radiohead and LCD Sound System top the lineup for the 2016 edition, with Lana Del Rey and J. Cole following closely behind. Electronic music predictably sees a major distribution on the lineup as well, with names like Zedd, Major Lazer, Jauz and Thomas Jack finding themselves on every row on the way down.

Outside Lands has taken place since 2008 – and while previous editions graced locations like the Polo Fields and Hellman’s Hollow, this year’s edition will call Golden Gate Park home.

At that, the 2016 edition of Outside Lands is scheduled for August 5th – 7th. Look through the lineup announcement below and let us know if you plan to attend by making a trip down to the comments section.
