Who is Don Lemon?

Born on March 1, 1966, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, Don Lemon has established himself as a prominent black journalist and television personality.

He started his career as a news reporter at local Louisiana stations before joining CNN in 2006.

Over the years, he has gained recognition for his sharp intellect, unwavering commitment to truth, and willingness to challenge the status quo.

Education and Early Career

After graduating from Baker High School in Baton Rouge, Lemon earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in Broadcast Journalism from Brooklyn College in New York City.

He developed his skills at local news stations in Alabama, Missouri, and Chicago before becoming part of WABC-TV in New York in 2003.

Don Lemon Vivek Ramaswamy: Rise to Prominence at CNN

After joining CNN in 2006 as a correspondent, Lemon rapidly distinguished himself.

In 2014, he assumed the role of co-anchor for CNN Tonight and later co-hosted CNN This Morning alongside Poppy Harlow from 2018 to 2023.

His straightforward approach and insightful commentary garnered him a devoted following, solidifying his position as a leading voice in American journalism.

Don Lemon’s Net Worth

Lemon has amassed a significant net worth, estimated to be around $12 million. This wealth is attributed to his successful career in journalism, including his lucrative contract with CNN.

Personal Life

Lemon is openly gay and has been involved in various LGBTQ+ advocacy efforts. He married his longtime partner, Tim Malone, in 2019. Despite his public profile, he maintains a relatively private personal life.

Don Lemon Vivek Ramaswamy: Ramaswamy’s Rising Star in Conservative Politics

After graduating with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Biology from Harvard University and earning a Juris Doctor degree from Yale Law School, Ramaswamy entered the business world.

He co-founded Roivant Sciences, a healthcare company, and subsequently launched Strive Asset Management, an investment firm, marking successful milestones in his entrepreneurial career.
